WHAT IS HACKER FELLOWS?

Hacker Fellows is a one-year fellowship program where recent Computer Science college graduates work as a developer at a Michigan start-up. Hacker Fellows start with 5 weeks of cohort-based training before diving into Michigan start-up. Hacker Fellows are an elite group of developers passionate about making an impact through tech. Cool, huh?

BOOTCAMP

An elite group of selected fellows will train in the Grand Circus classroom space in downtown Detroit over the course of five weeks. During this time, Hacker Fellows will acquire the entrepreneurial and technical skills needed to work for an amazing start-up in Michigan. Fellows will also develop a strong cohort of peers and start-up junkies. By being immersed in the start-up ecosystem, Fellows will create relationships and develop a firm understanding of the business side of start-ups.

WORKING AT A STARTUP

Fellows will be thrown into the deep end and given the opportunity to make a huge impact on the high-growth high-demand tech sector. Fellows will be matched with a Michigan start-up following training and work as a developer for the remainder of the year-long fellowship. Hacker Fellows will also participate in ongoing mentorship and activities throughout the year. This program will provide the support required to create a "soft-landing" for recent Computer Science graduates coming to new cities in Michigan.
