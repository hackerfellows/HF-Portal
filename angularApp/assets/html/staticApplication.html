PAYOFF

Hacker Fellows addresses the #1 problem tech start-ups in Michigan report: finding top developer talent for companies. The primary goals of Hacker Fellows are:

  • Attract tech entrepreneurs to Michigan start-ups
  • Promote job creation within the tech community
  • Fill the void of developers among Michigan start-ups and tech communities
  • Create a platform for mentoring, relationships and collaboration within the tech community
  • Boost Hacker Fellows compensation by ~$20,000 to help attract the top talent

REQUIREMENTS

  • Backed by a venture capital company or angel investor

  • Have substantial operations in Michigan

  • Early stage start-up (less than 5 years old)

  • For profit business

  • Doing cool techy things, commercializing innovative technology

  • Willing to commit to hiring and keeping a computer science major over the one-year fellowship
